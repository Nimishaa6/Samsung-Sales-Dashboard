# Samsung-Sales-Dashboard

This project is an interactive Samsung Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze sales performance from 2021–2024. It provides insights into revenue, product performance, regional sales, and customer behavior through dynamic visualizations and KPIs. The dashboard helps identify top-performing products, regions, and customer segments for data-driven decision-making. It was developed using Power BI, DAX, Power Query, and Excel for data transformation, modeling, and visualization. The project demonstrates practical Business Intelligence and Data Analytics skills in a real-world business scenario. 🚀

# 📊 Key KPIs

* 💰 **Total Revenue:** **$22.9M** (2021–2024)
* 📦 **Units Sold:** **36,580**
* 🛒 **Average Order Value (AOV):** **$626.3**
* 🎯 **Average Discount:** **5.7%**
* ⭐ **Average Customer Rating:** **3.74 / 5.0**
* 🌍 **Top Region:** **Europe** ($7.9M Revenue, 34.5% Share)
* 🏆 **Top Product:** **Samsung Neo QLED 8K QN900C**
* 🇬🇷 **Top Country:** **Greece** ($552K Revenue)
* 🌏 **Asia Revenue:** **$6.7M** (29.4% of Total Revenue)
* 📱 **Samsung Loyal Customers:** **8.7%**
* 🔄 **iOS Switchers:** **7.26%**
* 💳 **Top Payment Method:** **Samsung Pay** ($2.99M)
* 📈 **Customer Return Rate:** **9.21%**
* ✅ **Product Retention Rate:** **85.9%**

### Business Insights

* Smart TVs generated the highest category revenue (**$5.6M**).
* E-commerce Platform was the leading sales channel.
* Non-5G devices contributed over **62%** of yearly sales.
* Government segment generated the highest customer-segment revenue (**$4.75M**).
* Customers aged **25–34** represented the largest purchasing group.

# 📌 Final Conclusion

This Samsung Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of business performance across revenue, products, regions, and customer behavior from 2021–2024. By combining Executive Overview, Regional & Product Deep Dive, and Customer Intelligence, the dashboard enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions quickly and effectively.

Key findings reveal that **Europe is the highest-performing region**, **Smart TVs are the top revenue-generating category**, and the **Government segment contributes the highest customer-segment revenue**. The analysis also highlights customer preferences, payment trends, return rates, and the growing adoption of Samsung products across different markets.

Through interactive visualizations and KPIs, this dashboard demonstrates how Business Intelligence can transform raw sales data into actionable insights, helping organizations optimize strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and drive sustainable growth.

### 🎯 Project Outcome

* Improved visibility into sales performance and trends.
* Identified top-performing products, regions, and customer segments.
* Enabled data-driven decision-making through interactive analytics.
* Showcased the power of Power BI for business reporting and visualization.
